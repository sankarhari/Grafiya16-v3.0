Welcome to SMK Fomra

On stepping into the campus of Shree Motilal Kanhaiyalal Fomra Institute Of Technology, the students enter into the world of true dreams. The college is spread over an area of 25 acres of land. The College has spacious Lecture Halls, Computer Labs, Electrical Lab, Electronic Devices Lab, Electronic Circuit and Control Lab, Microwave Lab, RF Lab, DSP Lab,

Physics And Chemistry Labs, Drawing Hall, Workshop, Central Library, Canteen, Hostels (Boys And Girls) And Administrative Office. All infrastructure facilities meet the requirements prescribed as per the AICTE/ Anna University norms. Separate waiting halls/ rest rooms are provided for the boys and girls. Staying in the college campus is exciting and dynamic as students from various regions, background, culture and languages share their views of diversity in a united forum. The students learn from each other to develop a feeling of oneness and memorable friendship, an experience in a life-time and memory to be cherished throughout their life. Counselors advise the students with career choices and employment opportunities. Faculty helps in educational, mental, moral and emotional development and guide them to become good professionals.
